Our Menu

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, and is traditionally prepared with steamed milk foam.

5.99$

Frappuccino

A frappuccino is a blended coffee drink that is made with coffee, ice, and other ingredients, such as flavored syrups and whipped cream.

5.99$

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee drink that is made by forcing pressurized hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

5.99$

Latte

A latte is a coffee drink that is made with espresso and steamed milk. It is similar to a cappuccino, but has more milk and less foam.

5.99$

Mocha

A mocha is a chocolate-flavored coffee drink that is made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup.

5.99$

Macchiato

A macchiato is a coffee drink that is made with espresso and a small amount of milk.

5.99$

Americano

An americano is a coffee drink that is made with espresso and hot water. It is similar to drip coffee, but has a stronger flavor.

5.99$

Flat White

A flat white is a coffee drink that is made with espresso and steamed milk. It is similar to a latte, but has less milk and more foam.

5.99$

Irish Coffee

An irish coffee is a coffee drink that is made with hot coffee, irish whiskey, and sugar, and is topped with whipped cream.

5.99$

Turkish Coffee

A turkish coffee is a coffee drink that is made by boiling finely ground coffee beans in water, and is traditionally served in a small cup.

5.99$
